Plastic box:


HS Code: 3926.90.90.90
HTS Code:

Description:
Other articles of plastics and articles of other materials of headings 3901 to 3914.
This is a broad category that includes various plastic items not specifically classified elsewhere within Chapter 39 of the Harmonized System (HS) codes.




Indonesian Authority:
Directorate General of Customs and Excise (DGCE)

- under the Ministry of Finance
- is primarily responsible for controlling and managing HS (Harmonized System) codes.


HS vs HTS Code:

HTS = Harmonized Tariff Schedule codes are country-specific.
- ranges from 7 - 10 digits
HS = Harmonized System codes are internationally standardized.
- 6 digit number universal to all countries under International Harmonized System.
