1. EP4341623 A1: Container for phase-change material (US Pat)


Abstract:

The present disclosure relates to a container (100) for a phase change material, wherein said container is characterized in that it comprises:
- a closed shell (101) where a filling opening (103) is arranged;
- a phase change material nested in said shell (101);
- one or several recesses (105) designed to receive a conduit for refrigerant fluid.

Questions:

2. what are the materials the phase change material is made of?
