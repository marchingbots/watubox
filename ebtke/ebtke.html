Direktorat Jenderal Energi Baru, Terbarukan dan Konservasi Energi
Directorate General of New, Renewable Energy and Energy Conservation


EBTKE is the Directorate General within the Indonesian Ministry of Energy and Mineral Resources (MEMR) that is responsible for developing, controlling, and supervising activities related to new and renewable energy sources, as well as energy conservation efforts.

EBTKE is the government body in Indonesia focused on promoting and implementing policies related to:
1. New and Renewable Energy: This includes geothermal, bioenergy, and other forms of sustainable energy sources.
2. Energy Conservation: This involves efforts to reduce energy consumption and improve energy efficiency.

One of its initiatives is reducing emissions from fossil fuel burning.
