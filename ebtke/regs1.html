what is HS code 8415.10.10?		 A single split wall-mounted air conditioner consists of one indoor unit mounted on a wall and one outdoor unit. These units are connected by refrigerant lines and electrical wiring, allowing the indoor unit to cool a single room or space.
=Inverter and non-inverter air conditioners differ primarily in how they regulate temperature. Non-inverter models use a fixed-speed compressor that turns on and off to maintain temperature, while inverter models use a variable-speed compressor that adjusts its speed to match the cooling demand, leading to greater energy efficiency and more consistent temperature
HS code 8415.10.10 refers to self-contained air conditioning machines designed to be fixed to a window, wall, ceiling, or floor. These machines can be either self-contained or split-system and include elements for changing temperature and humidity, even if humidity cannot be separately regulated.
